  ## Changelog

  Nijta’s Voice Harbor platform offers a comprehensive data privacy compliance solution designed specifically for healthcare AI companies. Our platform empowers organizations to collect, process, and share sensitive patient communications—including calls, audio recordings, transcripts, and documents containing Protected Health Information (PHI)—in full compliance with HIPAA guidelines. By securing your data with robust privacy measures, Voice Harbor enables you to unlock the immense value hidden in your communications while streamlining front-office healthcare operations such as appointment scheduling, emergency call redirection, patient follow-ups, and doctor-patient conversation summaries.

  #### Features

  * Platform
  * PII and biometric redaction
